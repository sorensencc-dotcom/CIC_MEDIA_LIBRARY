FORD MOTOR COMPANY
River Rouge Plant, Dearborn, Michigan

INTER-OFFICE MEMORANDUM

LOR Charles Emil Sorensen

FROM: Production Planning Department

DATE: March 14, 1943

RE: Willow Run B-24 Output Targets

Mr. Sorensen,

As discussed with Henry Ford and the War Production Boar
output at Willow Run is expected to reach 650 aircraft ps

month by June 1943. Current rate is 405 units.

Harry Bennett has approved the shift schedule revision.
The UAW contract amendment is pending Board review.

Please advise on the Ypsilanti workforce expansion plan.

Respectfully,
Ford Motor Company
